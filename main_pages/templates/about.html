Presentation

SpaceX is the first travel agency to offer public space trips. In close collaboration with all major aerospace agencies, we are able to coordinate various types of trips to space for both individuals and scientists. We also offer gravity-free flights to introduce passengers to microgravity without leaving the earth.

History

SpaceX Inc. is a joint-venture between the NASA, the ESA and Spacecraft Technologies Corp. It was launched in 1998 to promote microgravity as a scientific research tool. It was first initiated by a group of astronauts from both the ESA and the NASA to provide gravity-free flights both in the US and Europe. In 2017 they took a strategic move and decided to launch a joint-venture with the company Spacecraft Technologies Corp. to create the first ever space travel agency.
