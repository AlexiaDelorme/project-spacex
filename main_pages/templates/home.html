Book your first trip to Space

SpaceX is the first travel agency that will fly you to space. This is not science fiction, this is technology and innovation meeting reality. In close collaboration with all major aerospace agencies in the world, we are able to coordinate various types of trips to space.

Trips for individuals

We organize various trips several times a year to bring people and (not just astronauts) to explore different parts of our solar system. Most of these trips are operated from various bases such as the Kennedy Space Center in the US. If you want to experience gravity-free environement without going to space, we also offer a wide range of parabolic flights from various airports across the world.

Trips for scientists

We can organize tailor-made scientifc flights to assist you in your scientific research. Weightlessness is an incredibly innovative tool to enhance fundamental knowledge in various disciplines such as physics, medicine, biology... We can assist you in your scientific experiences by setting up the logistic you need to be in a microgravity environement be it through parabolic flights or real space trips. These services can significantly contribute to the expansion of our knowledge in many scientific disciplines.
