Our main activity is to organise scientific research trips to space as well as scientific parabolic flights for research in gravity or microgravity.

Examples of experiments

  • Quantum physics
  • Medicine
  • Equivalence principles

Process for research trips/flights

  1. Application
  2. Selection process
  3. Experiment development
  4. Tests and validation
  5. Trip preparation

Gravity or microgravity?

  • Advantages of spacecraft: long weightlessness periods, human subject research, microgravity level 10 -6 g.
  • Disadvantages of spacecraft: expensive, complex procedures, technical constraints, long time before retrieving experiments results.
  • Advantages of aircraft: limited cost, easy to use, limited technical constraints, faster procedures.
  • Disadvantages of aircraft: fractioned weightlessness period, microgravity level around 10-2 g.
